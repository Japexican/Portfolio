About Me

Hello, my name is Sebastian Doreste. I am from Auburn, Alabama. I have always loved anything to do with technology, but never knew how to truely make it into a career. Recently I decided to stop wasting my life away in places I was not happy and start a new career doing something I love.

To make my dream come true I decided to attend the University of Central Florida to pursue their full stack web development course. By the end of the course I will be adept in HTML, CSS, Git, Github, JavaScript, Bootstrap, jQuery, AJAX, Node, Express, MySQL, Handlebars, Sequelize, Heroku, MongoDB, and React.

I enjoy taking on challenging tasks and solving them quickly and efficiently. I am very motivated and willing to learn. I am excited to start a new career, collaborate with my peers to achieve our goals, and learn new skills that will enable me to grow and become a great asset to any team I work with.
